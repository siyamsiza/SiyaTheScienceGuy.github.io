About Me

Siyabonga Msiza

I am an aspiring data scientist with a passion for using data to solve real-world problems, particularly in the financial sector. My journey began with an interest in artificial intelligence and a desire to develop intelligent systems that make people’s lives easier and more efficient. Over the past year, I have honed my skills through online courses, webinars, and practical experience, including participation in Kaggle competitions and personal projects, such as developing machine learning models for Housing and Stock price predictions.

I studied for a Bachelor of Sciences degree at the University of Cape Town majoring in Applied Statistics. I am particularly focused on building machine learning models for algorithmic trading, portfolio optimization, and financial forecasting. My recent projects include a Stock Price Dashboard and a Black-Scholes Option Pricing Dashboard, both hosted on Streamlit. These projects integrate real-time data extraction and advanced financial models, reflecting my drive to merge data science and finance in impactful ways.
